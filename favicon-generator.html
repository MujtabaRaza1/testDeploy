How to Create Your Favicon

Step 1: Prepare Your Logo

I've copied your WeFleet logo to assets/wefleet-logo-orig.png to preserve the original.

Step 2: Generate Favicon

Go to one of these free favicon generator sites:

  • RealFaviconGenerator (recommended)
  • Favicon.cc
  • Favicon.io

Upload your logo file and download the generated favicon package.

Step 3: Implement the Favicon

Once you have the favicon.ico file:

  1. Place the favicon.ico file in your assets directory
  2. Your HTML files already have the correct link tag:
    3. <link rel="shortcut icon" href="assets/favicon.ico" type="image/x-icon">

Step 4: Advanced Implementation (Optional)

For the best cross-browser support, favicons can include multiple sizes and formats. Add these to the head section of your HTML files:

<!-- Standard favicon -->
<link rel="icon" type="image/x-icon" href="assets/favicon.ico">

<!-- iOS/macOS icons -->
<link rel="apple-touch-icon" sizes="180x180" href="assets/favicon/apple-touch-icon.png">

<!-- Android/Chrome icons -->
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="32x32" href="assets/favicon/favicon-32x32.png">
<link rel="icon" type="image/png" sizes="16x16" href="assets/favicon/favicon-16x16.png">

<!-- Web App Manifest -->
<link rel="manifest" href="assets/favicon/site.webmanifest">
