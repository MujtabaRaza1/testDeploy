Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the basic maintenance checks?

+

Basic maintenance checks include fluid level inspections, tire pressure checks, brake pad measurements, and general vehicle safety assessments.

Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

+

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The new rate will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

What is the response time for emergency services?

+

Our average response time is 30 minutes or less in metropolitan areas. Response times may vary in rural locations.

Do you offer custom plans for larger fleets?

+

Yes, we offer customized enterprise solutions for fleets larger than 50 vehicles. Contact our sales team for a tailored quote.
